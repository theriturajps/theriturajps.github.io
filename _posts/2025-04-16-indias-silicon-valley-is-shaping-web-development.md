Bangalore, often called the **"Silicon Valley of India"**, is a hotbed for JavaScript innovation. With over **1,000+ tech startups** and global giants like Google, Microsoft, and Infosys headquartered here, the demand for JavaScript expertise is skyrocketing.  

In this article, we’ll explore:  
- Why Bangalore is a **JavaScript powerhouse**  
- Top **JS frameworks** used by Bangalore’s developers  
- Best **meetups and communities** to join  
- **Career opportunities** and salaries  
- How to **upskill** in JavaScript locally  

## Why Bangalore Loves JavaScript  

### 1. The Startup Boom  
Bangalore’s startup ecosystem thrives on **agile web development**, and JavaScript’s versatility makes it the #1 choice. Companies like **Flipkart, Swiggy, and Razorpay** rely on **React, Node.js, and Angular** for seamless user experiences.  

### 2. Global Tech Giants  
Microsoft’s Bangalore office contributes to **TypeScript** (a JS superset), while **Google’s Firebase** is widely adopted for backend solutions.  

### 3. Strong Developer Community  
- **JS Bangalore Meetup** (5,000+ members)  
- **React India Conference** (Annual flagship event)  
- **Bangalore Node.js User Group**
